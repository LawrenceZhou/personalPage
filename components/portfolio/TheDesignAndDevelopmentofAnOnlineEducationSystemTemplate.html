The Design And Development of An Online Education System
The continuous development of information technology in learning has been promoting the integration and development of internet industry and education sector. On-line-education system, as the inevitable trend of the development of education sector in the internet era, has never failed to get enough attention.

In this project, the development of on-line-education system based on learning management system is a simple, flexible, and efficient way, which also provides live and video on demand features for users. The overall system design is based on an open source project, Moodle. The method to design and implement live and video on demand(VOD) features, which is a secondary development of the system, is using the WebRTC media server, Kurento. This is my bachelor graduation project, as part of the Intel China OES project.
