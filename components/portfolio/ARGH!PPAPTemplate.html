ARGH! PPAP
A Voice-Control Virtual Reality Game
ARGH! PPAP! is a first-person-shooter VR game that is controlled by the player using simple audio input through the microphone. The player would be placed in a wondrous world where there are myriad fruits of three kinds floating in the air: apple, pineapple, and orange. The goal of the player is to control the character direction to aim and shoot pens at the correct fruits.
Motivation
Tired of keyboard input game.
In the first prototype, ARGH! PPAP! is a shooting game that is controlled by the direct keyboard input, which is old-fashioned and tedious. I want a new way to play the game! Maybe voice is a good choice?
PC game is not immersive.
Get enough of looking around by pressing "WASD"? Why not try the VR headset?
PPAP is a big hit!
PPAP is a big hit by Piko Taro in 2017. Will it be chemistrty to integrate the game with the elements of this song?
Gameplay
  • Aim at the apple, pineapple, or orange, as these fruits are rewarding; try to avoid aiming at watermelon or papaya, as these fruits are unrewarding.
  • Shoot a pen by shouting out "ARGH". You have to shout it out loud enough, and the higher pitch you are shouting, the faster the pen is.
  • Shoot an apple and followed by a pineapple, you will get an Area of Effect(AOE) pen.
  • Fruits are fluctuating faster as the game goes on, which makes it harder.
  • ARGH! PPAP is a super self-explanatory game, check the demo video below.
