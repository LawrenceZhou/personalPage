Tasks

  • Complex: Find people with common interests/goals, and ward off their direct attention
Storyboard
Find people with common interests/goals, and ward off their direct attention: Lonely person is forlornly looking at online pictures of people having a great time with friends. The person is summoned to join two people he has never met in person.

Find a way to approach new companions: App sets up game. Lonely person and friends all travel around campus, doing fun Mario-Party-esque challenges. After discovering their common interest (music), they play instruments together.

Make new friends: The three friends talk and laugh at a table. Two of them get up and start walking down a path into the sunset, and spontaneously start holding hands.
